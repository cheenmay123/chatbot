

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Anagni Sveda Aatapa Sevana





Chikitsaa Prakaara: Anagni
Sveda  Aatapa Sevana

Prakaara
Paribhaashaa: 
This is a
type of Anagni Svedana.
Aatapa Sevana means exposure
to sunrays.

Kaala/Vaya/Ritu/Ahoraatra:
This
procedure should be done during Hemanta
Ritu as a daily regime to maintain health.

Chikitsaa Karma:
Aggravates Pitta Dosha and Rakta
Dhaatu and alleviates Kapha Dosha
& Vaata Dosha. 
Sweating
occurs due to this.
