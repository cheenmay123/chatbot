

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Mrita Sanjeevana




Chikitsaa Prakaara: Mrita
Sanjeevana

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
 Chakrapaan`ee.
The medicine and measures
used to resuscitate the patient from the shock due to poison is termed as Mrita Sanjeevana.

Pradhaanakarma:
Mrita Sanjeevana Agada should be used for Nasya, Anjana, etc.
