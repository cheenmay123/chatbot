Udarda

Reference -
B.R.
55, Y.R.
Uttaraardha S`heetapittodardakot`ha Chikitsaa, N.R. S`heetapitta

Introduction:
This is a S`hotha in the shape of conch shell.
There is Kan`d`oo, Toda, Chhardi, Jvara, Vidaaha. This is called as Udarda. Its another name is S`heetapitta. 
Udarda is
Kaphaja and it is caused due to cold
stuffs. (S`his`hiraja Vikaara)
S`heetapitta is Vaata dominant
and Udarda is Kapha dominant.
