Visphot`a Chikitsaa

Reference: 
Y.R.(Uttaraardha), B.R., N.R. Visphot`a

Introduction:
The blisters
which resemble to the blisters due to burn and manifests all over the body are
caused due to Rakta and Pitta. They are called as Visphot`a.
