Atatvaabhinives`ha Chikitsaa

Reference
C.Chi.10

Introduction
Literally, Atatvaabhinives`ha means feeling,
affection, determination or adherence to the non-existing things. This is a psychological
disorder. It is difficult to treat and due to its nature it is known as Mahaagada.
