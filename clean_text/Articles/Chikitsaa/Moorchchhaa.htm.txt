Introduction:
It is also known as
fainting. It is caused by Pitta and Tama. Pitta is in dominance in all types of Moorchchhaa. It is of following types

Madyaja

Aavasthika Chikitsaa

Prasakta Vega Avasthaa

Mukha-Naasaavarodhana (Close the orifice of mouth and nose) Followed by Nasya, S`hamana

Based on the predominance of the Dosha the treatment should be
     adopted accordingly.
Satvaavajaya
     Chikitsaa should be adopted
     as and when necessary.
Chikitsaa Similar to - Sannyaasa in Pravridha
     Avasthaa

If
this is due to Visha, then Visha Chikitsaa should be adopted.
