Chikitsaa Prakaara: Dhoopana
(Fumigation)

Prakaara
Paribhaashaa:
Fumigation is termed
as Dhoopana.

Poorvakarma (Prerequisites):
Medicine preparation: 
Dhoopana Dravya are S`hreevesht`aka,
Sarjarasa, Sarala, Devadaaru, Kshauma, Yava, Sarpi, Guggulu, Vachaa, Nimba

Chikitsaa Karma:
Prevents Kan`d`oo, Paaka and Raaga. 

Other Description:
Indications can be
got through Anveshak search facility.
