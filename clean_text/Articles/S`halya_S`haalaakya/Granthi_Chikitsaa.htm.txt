Introduction:
Granthi
is a localized growth manifesting with a symptom S`hotha and which resembles a bunch or protuberance of any kind. 

Saamaanya

There are chances of
formation of Granthi again; if
cauterization is done improperly (Asamyak
Dagdha), or if a surgeon keeps remnants at the time of removal of Granthi. Threfore, for a surgeon it is
necessary to remove Granthi with its
capsule (Kos`ha). Afterwards, it is
to be treated as Vran`a.
