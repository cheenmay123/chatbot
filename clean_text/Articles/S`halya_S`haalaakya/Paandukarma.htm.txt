Chikitsaa Prakaara: Paan`d`ukarma

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
The procedure by which the black scar on
the skin is transformed to the skin colour is termed as Paan`d`ukarma.

Pradhaanakarma:
Basically this comprises the post procedure of Vran`a Karma.
This should be done on blackish scar formed on the skin after the wound
heals in fair skin people.
Indications and
various formulations (Lepa etc.) used
can be got through Anveshak search
facility.
