

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Roma Sanjanana





Chikitsaa Prakaara: Roma
Sanjanana

Prakaara
Paribhaashaa:
The procedure by
which hair growth is stimulated is termed as Roma Sanjanana.

Pradhaanakarma:
This Chikitsaa Prakaara is explained in context to Vran`a.
Sometimes it happens that after wound healing, there is no hair growth at
that site. To stimulate the hair growth
various Lepa are applied.
Indications and
various formulations (Lepa etc.) used
can be got through Anveshak search
facility.
