

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Bid`aalaka




Chikitsa Prakaara:
Bid`aalaka

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
D`alhan`a.
The Lepa which is applied around the eyeball
excluding the eyelids is termed as Bid`aalaka.

Pradhaanakarma:
While application
precaution should be taken to avoid spilling of medicine inside the eye.

Other Description:
Indications and
various formulations used can be got through Anveshak search facility.
