Chikitsaa Prakaara: Krishn`ikaran`a

Prakaaqra Paribhaashaa:
The procedure by which the white scar on
the skin is transformed to the skin colour is termed as Krishn`ikaran`a.

Pradhaanakarma:
Basically this comprises of the post procedure of Vran`a Karma.
In dark people this should be done on the whitish scar formed on the skin
after healing of the wound
