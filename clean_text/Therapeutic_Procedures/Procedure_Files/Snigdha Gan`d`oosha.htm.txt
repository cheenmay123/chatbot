

Chikitsaa Prakaara:Snigdha Gan`d`oosha



Chikitsaa Prakaara:Snigdha
Gan`d`oosha

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
Gan`d`oosha done with the help of unctuous medicine.

Other Description:
This type of Gan`d`oosha should be done by drugs
having Svaadu (sweet), Amla (sour) and Lavan`a (salty) taste.
Gan`d`oosha is contraindicated in children below the age of 5 years.
