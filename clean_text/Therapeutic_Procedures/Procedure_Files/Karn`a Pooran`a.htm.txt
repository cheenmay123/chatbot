

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Karn`a Pooran`a



Chikitsaa Prakaara: Karn`a
Pooran`a

Prakaara
Paribhaashaa:
Administration
of unctuous drug inside the ear is termed as Karn`a Pooran`a.

Pradhaanakarma:
The medicine should be held in ear for100 Maatraa Kaala.

Other Description:
Precaution
should be taken not to administer Karn`a
Pooran`a if there is fungal infection in the ear.
Indications
and various formulations used can be got through Anveshak search facility.
