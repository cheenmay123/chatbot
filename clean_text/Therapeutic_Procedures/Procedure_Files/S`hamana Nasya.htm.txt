Chikitsaa Prakaara: S`hamana
Nasya

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
The
Nasya which helps to alleviate vitiated Dosha without physically eliminating them is termed as S`hamana Nasya.

Other Description:

Indications and various oils used
     can be got through Anveshak search
     facility.
