

Chikitsaa Prakaara: Dhooma Nasya



Chikitsaa Prakaara: Dhooma
Nasya

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:
Medicated Dhooma is inhaled through nose and
expelled through mouth.

Other Description:
The smoke should never be exhaled by nose as it harms the vision.
Indications
and various formulations used can be got through Anveshak search facility.
