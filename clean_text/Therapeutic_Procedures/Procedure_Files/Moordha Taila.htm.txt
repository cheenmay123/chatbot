Chikitsaa Prakaara: Moordha
Taila

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:

Ayurveda S`habdakos`ha
Application
of oil over head region is termed as Moordha
Taila.

Pradhaanakarma:
There are four types of Moordha
Taila 
S`hirobyanga,

S`hirodhaaraa,
S`hirobasti and
S`hira Pichu

Chikitsaakarma:
Kesha Drid`hatva (strengthens
hair)
S`hira Kapaalaasthi Balya
(strengthens bones of head) 
Indriya Prasannatva 
Mukha Kaantivardhaka
Sukha Nidraa
